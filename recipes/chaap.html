Soya Chaap Afghani

Description

Afghani Soya Chaap Recipe

  • 500 gm soya chaap
  • 1/4 cup hung Curd
  • 2 tsp tandoori Masala
  • 1 tsp Ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp garam Masala
  • 1/4 tsp lalmirch powder
  • 1/4 tsp black Pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Butter as required

Method

  1. Boil water in a pan. Add soya chaap in it and boil it on low flame for 7-8 minutes. Drain the water properly.
  2. To marinate, take hung curd in a bowl add all the ingredients. Add boiled soya chaap.
  3. Marinate it for minimum 4-5 hours.
  4. Now, preheat air fryer at 180 C for 5 minutes.
  5. Put soya chaap in it and apply butter and air fry it at 180 C for 6-7 minutes. Turn the side and apply butter and air fry it for 6-7 minutes till crisp.
  6. Serve with green chutney.
